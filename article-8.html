The Future of Renewable Energy

By Linda Green | Published: Aug 30, 2025

Renewable energy is undergoing a transformation, driven by falling costs, global policy commitments, and urgent climate needs. Solar, wind, and hydroelectric power are expanding at record rates, while new technologies such as fusion energy and hydrogen storage are emerging as potential game-changers.

Experts predict that by 2040, renewables could provide the majority of global electricity demand. Countries are investing heavily in grid modernization, storage solutions, and smart infrastructure to make clean energy more reliable and accessible.

Mars Colonization: Next Giant Leap

By Robert White | Published: Aug 28, 2025

Human colonization of Mars is no longer a dream of science fiction. With private companies and national agencies making rapid progress in spacecraft design, sustainable habitats, and resource utilization, the possibility of establishing a permanent presence on the Red Planet draws nearer each year.

Missions planned for the late 2020s include not just exploration, but building infrastructure for long-term settlement. Technologies like in-situ resource utilization (ISRU) aim to produce fuel, oxygen, and water directly from Martian soil and atmosphere.
